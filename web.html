Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

About the Movie

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a 2013 Bollywood romantic drama directed by Ayan Mukerji and produced by Karan Johar. It stars Ranbir Kapoor as Bunny and Deepika Padukone as Naina.

The movie follows the story of a free-spirited traveler and his journey through friendship, love, and self-discovery.
